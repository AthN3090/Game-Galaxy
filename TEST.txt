Shadow of the Tomb Raider is an action-adventure video game 
developed by Eidos Montréal and published by Square Enix.
It continues the narrative from the 2015 game Rise of the 
Tomb Raider and is the twelfth mainline entry in the Tomb Raider
series. The game was released worldwide on 14 September 2018 
for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4 and Xbox One. A macOS and
Linux version of the game was released by Feral Interactive on
5 November 2019.
